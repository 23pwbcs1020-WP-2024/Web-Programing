Best Routine for Fitness


    

  • Incorporate Strength Training: Aim for at least two days of strength training
per week to build muscle and boost metabolism.
    • 

  • Mix Cardio and HIIT: Include both steady-state cardio and high-intensity interval
training for improved cardiovascular health and fat loss.
    • 

  • Prioritize Recovery: Ensure adequate rest and recovery days to prevent injury and
allow muscles to repair and grow.
